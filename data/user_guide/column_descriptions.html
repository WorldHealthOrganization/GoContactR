The analyses in this app rely on appropriately named columns. Your original columns may have been renamed internally. Check that the final dataframe shown contains at least the following columns

  • id: A unique ID for each case

  • id_contact: A unique ID for each contact

  • date du dernier contact: The date of last contact between the case and the contact

  • region: Admin level 1 of the contact

  • district: Admin level 2 of the contact

  • follow up date:Dates of follow-up for monitored contacts

  • etat suivi: Follow-up status for each day that contacts are monitored
