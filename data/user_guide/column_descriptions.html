The analyses in this app rely on appropriately named columns. If you are uploading a csv or xlsx file, it should have at least the columns below (capital, small letters or title case are all fine):

  • id: A unique ID for each case

  • id_contact: A unique ID for each contact

  • date enreg: Date of contact registration

  • date du dernier contact: The date of last contact between the case and the contact

  • prefecture: Admin level 1 of the contact

  • sous-prefecture: Admin level 2 of the contact

  • j1, j2 ...j21: follow-up status for each day that contacts are monitored
